Common Questions

Quick answers for referring providers

What is the time commitment?

+

Sessions last 3-20 minutes, daily (Monday-Friday) for 36 treatments. The first 30 are daily, with the final 6 tapered over 3 weeks.

What are the side effects?

+

Most common is mild headache (50%) or scalp tapping sensations during treatment. Serious risks like seizure are exceedingly low (<0.5%).

Can patients continue their current medications?

+

Yes, most medications can continue. However, minimize benzodiazepines as they may decrease TMS efficacy.

What if my patient can't come 5 days per week?

+

Minimum 3 sessions weekly required. Below this frequency, efficacy drops significantly per clinical guidelines.

Who manages overall psychiatric care during TMS?

+

You do. The referring provider maintains primary psychiatric care including medication management and crisis intervention.

What happens after treatment ends?

+

Follow-up at our continuity clinic 1-3 months after completion. Ongoing care continues under your management.
